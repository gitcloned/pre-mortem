What if your project failed?

Pre-mortem helps teams surface risks before they happen — async input, team voting, clear action items.

How it works

Four steps to a safer project

1

Create a session

Facilitator creates a pre-mortem session and shares the link with the team.

2

Team adds risks

Everyone submits what they think could go wrong — privately, at their own pace.

3

Reveal & vote

Facilitator reveals all risks. Team votes on the ones that matter most.

4

Assign & export

Top risks get owners and remarks. Export everything to your task tool.
